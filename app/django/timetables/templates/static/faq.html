Timetable Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export my timetable to my Google Calendar?

  1. Log into your Google Calendar
  2. Click the arrow button next to "Other calendars" and select "Add by URL"
  3. Copy and paste the link above into the "URL" box
  4. Click "Add Calendar"
  5. Your personal timetable is now displayed in your Google Calendar

Why are my events shown at different times in Timetable and my own calendar?

You'll need to check the timezone of your calendar. If you live outside the UK, you may well have your Google or other calendar set to the timezone in which you normally live. This means all the events will show up as being at the wrong time. You can reset your calendar's timezone to match the timezone Cambridge is in.

Why aren't changes to my Timetable showing up in my (Google) calendar?

Depending on which electronic calendar client you're using, and on its settings, it can take up to 24 hours for changes you make to your personal timetable to show up in your own calendar. For example, Google Calendar only updates every 4-6 hours.

If you have reset your calendar feed using the 'reset feed' link, your calendar won't receive updates, because it can no longer communicate with the Timetable system. You will need to add your new feed to your calendar client in order to see changes.
